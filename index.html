About Me

My name is Kevin Phan and I was born and raised in Arizona. I completed my undergraduate at Arizona State University in 2019 with a degree in Health Sciences (Pre-Professional). I am currently enrolled in the University of Arizona coding bootcamp to obtain a certificate in full-stack web development. Being raised by immigrant parents that came to America during extreme strife, much of my work ethic comes from them. Nothing in life comes for free and I want all of my works and projects to reflect the hard work and effort I put into them. One of the biggest goals for the future is to be able to integrate my knowledge gained from studying health sciences into different web pages and applications.
